Our Comprehensive Pet Care Services

Greendot Veterinary Consult offers a wide range of services to keep your pets healthy and happy. Our experienced team uses the latest veterinary practices and technology to provide the best possible care.

Veterinary Checkups

Regular wellness exams are crucial for early detection of potential health issues. We provide thorough physical examinations, tailored to your pet's age, breed, and lifestyle.

Vaccinations & Deworming

Protect your pet from common and serious infectious diseases with a personalized vaccination schedule. We also offer comprehensive deworming programs.

Pet Grooming

Our grooming services include baths, haircuts, nail trims, and ear cleaning, helping your pet look and feel their best while promoting skin and coat health.

Pet Health Consultations

We offer expert advice on nutrition, behavior, dental care, and weight management to help you make informed decisions about your pet's well-being.

Surgery & Diagnostics

Our clinic is equipped for a variety of surgical procedures, from routine spaying/neutering to more complex operations. We also offer in-house diagnostic services.

Pet Supplies

Visit our in-clinic pet shop for a curated selection of high-quality pet food, treats, accessories, grooming items, and health products recommended by our vets.
