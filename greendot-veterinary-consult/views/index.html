Welcome to Greendot Veterinary Consult

At Greendot Veterinary Consult, we are dedicated to providing the highest level of veterinary medicine along with friendly, compassionate service. We believe in treating every patient as if they were our own pet, and giving them the same loving attention and care. We are a group of highly trained, experienced animal lovers who are devoted to giving our patients the best care possible.

Located in the heart of Benin City, Nigeria, we offer a comprehensive range of services from routine checkups and vaccinations to advanced diagnostics and surgical procedures. Our modern clinic is equipped to ensure your pet receives top-quality care in a comfortable and welcoming environment.
