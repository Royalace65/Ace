About Greendot Veterinary Consult

Our Mission

At Greendot Veterinary Consult, our mission is to provide compassionate, comprehensive, and expert veterinary care to the beloved pets of Benin City. We strive to create a welcoming and supportive environment for both pets and their owners, fostering a partnership built on trust and a shared love for animals. We are committed to promoting responsible pet ownership and enhancing the human-animal bond through education and preventative care.

Our Story & Passion for Animals

Greendot Veterinary Consult was founded by a team of dedicated veterinary professionals who share a profound passion for animal welfare. Our journey began with a simple vision: to establish a clinic where every pet receives the highest standard of medical attention in a caring and gentle manner. We understand that pets are family, and we treat each patient with the same dedication and empathy as we would our own. Our love for animals is the driving force behind everything we do, from routine check-ups to complex medical treatments.

Meet Our Team

Dr. Ada Okoye

Lead Veterinarian, DVM

Dr. Okoye has over 10 years of experience in small animal medicine and surgery. Her special interests include internal medicine and preventative care. She is passionate about educating pet owners and ensuring the long-term health of her patients.

Mr. Ben Igbinosa

Head Veterinary Technician

Ben is a certified veterinary technician with a gentle touch and a deep love for all animals. He excels in patient care, assisting in surgeries, and making sure every pet feels comfortable and safe during their visit.

Ms. Chinyere Eze

Clinic Manager & Pet Supplies Advisor

Chinyere ensures the smooth operation of our clinic and is always ready to help clients with a smile. She also manages our pet shop, offering expert advice on nutrition and pet supplies.
